I am a PhD student in the Geoenergy and Geoenvironmental Engineering at Jackson School of Geosciences, The University of Texas at Austin . I obtained my M.Sc. and B.Sc. in Petroleum and Georesources Engineering.

My research focuses on subsurface energy storage. I work on CO2 storage, H2 storage, geothermal energy, critical mineral extraction from brine water, and geomechanics.

Primary fields: Reservoir simulation; coupled subsurface flow–geomechanics; fault leakage; caprock integrity; wellbore stability; containment risk; monitoring design; uncertainty analysis.

Secondary fields: Subsurface containment risk assessment; geologic energy storage design & monitoring; data integration.
